About Mandy

Hello and welcome! I am a qualified coach and mentor, with an ILM Level 7 Certificate for Executive and Senior Level Coaches and Mentors, as well as being a qualified practitioner of Neuro-Linguistic Programming. I also use my years of experience as an environmental trainer and facilitator, to offer excellent coaching, training and facilitation to individuals and groups in the public, private and voluntary sectors.

I have been working in the field of personal and professional development for 25 years, originally as a trainer, facilitator and coach at Losehill Hall, the Peak District National Park Authority’s Environmental Training Centre, and over the last 12 years have been successfully running my own business offering coaching, facilitation and training services.

Prior to this, I was a co-director, writer and performer for a theatre-in-education company, and earlier still, a registered nurse. Communicating well with all sorts of people is what my life’s work is about.

Facilitation

Good facilitation makes meetings, workshops and consultation events as enjoyable and effective as possible. If you have ever come away from a meeting involving a large group of people, feeling that there was a lack of clarity, that it was hard to participate, and that it was of little benefit, then it could probably have done with some expert facilitation.

I work as a facilitator for a great range of clients, and my aims are always the same: to ensure that the client’s objectives are achieved in the shortest time, with the optimum input from everyone and maximum enjoyment for all involved.

There are few trainers as gifted or as likely to make a significant impact as Mandy.  She wields an impressive array of training techniques and styles and does so in a way that engages with all attendees from the outset.
